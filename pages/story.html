Our aim is to offer an alternative view of eco-activism, one that
communicates the urgency of environmental issues through
the beauty and emotional power of art.

Through our eco-opera, we will take audiences on a captivating journey,
linking seemingly disparate locations and illustrating the deep
interconnection of natural systems and climate change challenges.

Our production seeks to bridge the gap between art and activism,
showing that environmental awareness can be evoked not through
destruction, but through a profound and tragic beauty.
